## 2. Information We Collect

We collect "Personal Data" (information that can identify you) and "Non-Personal Data" (information that cannot directly identify you). The following categories detail what we collect:

### A. Information You Provide Directly

- **Account Registration Data**: When you create an account or fill forms on 360Ghar, we ask for basic contact and profile details. This includes your full name, email address (optional), phone number, date of birth, gender, and password. We may also request your postal address or city to customize search results.
- **Identity and Verification Data (Property Owners)**: If you list a property with us, we require additional information to verify authenticity. This may include property ownership documents, title deed or registry copies, government-issued photo ID (e.g., PAN, Aadhaar, Passport), recent utility bills for address proof, and any relevant RERA registration number for new developments.
- **Property Listing Details**: Owners provide extensive information about their property: property type, address and exact location coordinates, photographs, videos or 360° tour imagery, floor plans, amenities, price, rental terms, maintenance fees, description, etc.
- **Profile and Preferences**: We may collect additional details when you fill out your profile or answer onboarding questions — for example, your property preferences (budget range, location preference, property type needed), family size, occupation, etc. Providing these is optional.
- **Communications with Us**: If you contact us (via support email, phone, chat, or ticket), we collect the information and any attachments you provide in those communications.
- **Feedback and Reviews**: If you participate in surveys, provide feedback, or review a property or our services, we collect your input which may include personal opinions and experiences.
- **Transactions and Payments**: Currently, for real estate brokerage transactions, payments (commission, etc.) are handled offline. If in the future we facilitate online payments, we or our payment gateway may collect your payment details such as billing address or partial credit card information.

### B. Information We Collect Automatically

- **Device and Log Information**: We log details about your usage of the Services, including your IP address, device type, device identifiers, operating system version, app version, browser type, pages or screens viewed, actions taken, and access times.
- **Location Data**: With your permission, we collect precise geolocation data of your device. This can help show your location relative to properties on the map or suggest nearby listings.
- **Cookies and Similar Tech**: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website to enhance user experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.
- **Analytics and Usage Data**: We use Firebase Analytics to collect information about user interactions with our Services, such as screen views and feature usage events.
- **Advertising Partners**: While currently 360Ghar does not show third-party ads, if in the future we partner with advertisers, they may use cookies or pixels to deliver relevant ads.

### C. Information from Third Parties

- If a friend refers you to our platform, we may receive your email or phone from them to send an invite.
- We may work with third-party data providers or marketing partners that provide demographic information or interest data.
- If you use a co-branded service or interact with a third-party service through our platform, those third parties might share back information with us about the interaction.

**Children's Data**: 360Ghar is not intended for minors. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18 years of age.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various lawful purposes:

- **To Provide and Improve Services**: We process data to operate our platform and deliver features to you.
- **Authentication and Security**: We use personal identifiers to verify accounts and to authenticate you at login.
- **Facilitating Transactions and Communication**: Your information will be used to facilitate communication between buyers and sellers with your consent.
- **Service Communication**: We will use your email and phone number to send administrative or service messages.
- **Marketing and Promotions**: We may use your contact details to send promotional content where you have not opted out. You can unsubscribe at any time.
- **Content Moderation and Enforcement**: Data such as your posted content and usage patterns may be reviewed to enforce our Terms and Content Guidelines.
- **Analytics and Personalization**: To enhance user experience, we analyze how different users interact with our app.
- **Map and Location-Based Services**: We use location data to provide map-based property discovery and driving directions.
- **Preventing Fraud/Illegal Activities**: We may use personal data to prevent, detect, and investigate fraud, security breaches, or other potentially prohibited or illegal activities.
- **Legal Compliance**: Any information may be used to comply with applicable legal obligations.

## 4. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We do not sell your personal data. However, we do share information in the following circumstances:

- **With Counterparties to Transactions**: When you express interest in a property or have an active deal, some information will be shared with the other party.
- **With Service Providers and Partners**: We employ trusted third-party companies and individuals to perform functions on our behalf.
- **Within Corporate Group**: Since 360Ghar is currently under YORD LLC (USA), we may share data among our parent, subsidiaries, and affiliates.
- **For Legal Requirements**: We may disclose personal information if required to do so by law.
- **During Business Transfers**: If 360Ghar or YORD LLC is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred as part of that deal.
- **With Consent**: We will share your personal information with third parties when we have your specific consent to do so.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required or permitted by law:

- **Account Data**: We keep your profile and account information while your account is active.
- **Transaction Data**: Information related to property transactions may be retained for longer periods due to legal and accounting obligations.
- **Content**: Posts or reviews you make may remain on the platform until you remove them.
- **Logs and Analytics**: Server logs and analytics data are typically retained for a shorter period (6–24 months).
- **Backups**: Your data might remain in secure backups for some additional time.

## 6. Data Security

We take reasonable and appropriate security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption, access controls, secure storage, monitoring and testing, training and policies, and incident response procedures.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights under applicable law:

- **Right to Access**: You have the right to know what personal data we hold about you and to obtain a copy.
- **Right to Correction**: If any of your personal information is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request correction or updating.
- **Right to Erasure**: You may request deletion of your personal data when it is no longer necessary for the purpose collected.
- **Right to Withdraw Consent**: For data processing based on your consent, you have the right to withdraw consent at any time.
- **Right to Data Portability**: You may have the right to request a copy of your personal data in a machine-readable format.
- **Right to Grievance Redressal**: If you have any grievances, you have the right to lodge a complaint with our Grievance Officer.
- **Right to Restrict or Object**: In certain situations, you may object to or ask us to limit processing of your data.

## 8. Content Removal and Takedown Requests
