## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Finding a flatmate should not feel like a gamble. Yet most of us scroll through endless listings, message strangers, and hope for the best. 360 FlatMates changes that by matching you with flatmates who actually fit your lifestyle — not just your budget.

SMART COMPATIBILITY MATCHING

Our 6-dimension compatibility engine scores every match across sleep schedules, cleanliness habits, food preferences, smoking and drinking attitudes, guest policies, and work styles. Every profile shows a compatibility percentage so you know before you message whether someone is your kind of person. No more guessing. No more awkward surprises after move-in.

FIND FLATS AND FLATMATES, YOUR WAY

Whether you are looking for a room, listing a spare one, or open to both — 360 FlatMates adapts to you. Choose your mode and the app reshapes around your needs. Room posters get a Post tab. Flat seekers get an Explore tab with an interactive map. Everyone gets the same powerful discovery tools.

DISCOVER AND EXPLORE

Browse curated listings personalized to your city, budget, and preferences. Search by location, area, or landmark. Filter by budget, room type, furnishing, gender preference, and move-in timeline. Explore listings on an interactive map with clustering so you can see what is available in your preferred neighborhood. Every listing shows rent, deposit, maintenance, amenities, photos, and video tours.

SWIPE TO MATCH

Like what you see? Swipe right to like, or swipe left to pass. When both sides like each other, it is a match. Start chatting instantly with built-in icebreakers or answer three quick Q&A questions to break the ice with more context. Your compatibility breakdown is always visible, so conversations start with understanding — not small talk.

CHAT WITH CONTEXT

Messages are private and built into the app. No need to share your phone number until you are ready. Send photos, see read receipts, and use icebreaker prompts when you do not know what to say. Conversation cards show the listing context so you always know which flat or person you are talking about.

SCHEDULE VISITS

Found a flat you like? Schedule a visit directly from the listing or conversation. Pick a date, choose a time slot, and add an optional note. Track all your upcoming, confirmed, and completed visits in one place.

LIST YOUR SPACE IN MINUTES

Got a spare room? Create a detailed listing with our step-by-step builder. Add room and flat details, amenities, photos, and video tours. Set rent, deposit, maintenance, and monthly costs. Describe your typical day and preferred flatmate. Listings are reviewed for quality and safety before going live, so every flatmate browsing your listing knows it is genuine.

SAFETY AND TRUST

Your safety is our priority. Phone numbers are verified at sign-in. Block or report anyone at any time. Listings go through a review process. Privacy controls let you hide your last name or exact location. Visit the room before you pay, and keep conversations in the app until you trust the person.

DESIGNED FOR INDIA

Available in English and Hindi. Works across Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, Chennai, Mumbai, and expanding. Light, dark, and system themes with palette options. Warm, editorial design that feels like home.

YOUR FLATMATE JOURNEY, SIMPLIFIED

1. Sign up with your phone number
2. Tell us what you are looking for
3. Take the lifestyle quiz
4. Set your budget and move-in timeline
5. Discover, swipe, match, and chat
6. Schedule a visit and find your home

Download 360 FlatMates and stop hoping for a good flatmate. Start matching with one.
