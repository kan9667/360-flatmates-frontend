## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Finding the right flatmate is hard. 360 FlatMates makes it easy.

Whether you're looking for a flat, listing a spare room, or searching for a flatmate to hunt for a place together — 360 FlatMates connects you with compatible people, not just listings.

**How it works:**

1. **Tell us who you are** — Set your mode: find a flat, list your flat, or both. Share your lifestyle preferences, budget, and deal-breakers so we find the right matches.

2. **Get matched** — Our compatibility engine scores flatmates across 6 dimensions: lifestyle, budget, cleanliness, sleep schedule, food habits, and social vibe. No more guessing if someone is a good fit.

3. **Swipe & connect** — Browse profiles, swipe on people who match your vibe, and start chatting when both sides are interested.

4. **Visit & decide** — Schedule flat visits directly in the app. Confirm times, add notes, and keep everything organized in one place.

5. **Live together, better** — From verified listings to compatibility scores, every feature is designed to help you find a flatmate you actually want to live with.

**Key features:**

- Compatibility scoring across 6 lifestyle dimensions
- Swipe-based flatmate discovery
- Verified listings with photos, videos, and detailed breakdowns
- In-app chat and icebreaker questions
- Visit scheduling and management
- Deal-breaker filtering so you never waste time
- Light/dark theme with palette switching
- Available in English and Hindi

360 FlatMates is built for India — for the 26-year-old moving to Bangalore, the student finding a flatmate in Pune, and the professional looking for someone who doesn't play music at 2 AM.

**Your next flatmate is already here.**
